What is the KEEP Protocol?

A comprehensive framework ensuring Bitcoin wealth transfers safely across generations

The KEEP Protocol represents the professional standard for Bitcoin estate planning, addressing the unique challenges of digital asset inheritance through a systematic approach that has been tested and refined with over 500 families.

Unlike traditional estate planning, Bitcoin requires specialized knowledge of cryptography, multi-signature security, and digital asset law. The KEEP Protocol provides the framework for professionals to deliver these services at the highest standard.

KEEP stands for:

  • K Key Management - Secure custody solutions
  • E Estate Integration - Legal framework alignment
  • E Education Systems - Family preparedness
  • P Professional Network - Certified expertise

Why KEEP Protocol?
