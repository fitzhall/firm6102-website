The Nightmare Scenario: Your Backup Fails

You've done everything right. You have a multi-signature wallet and you've backed up your seed phrases. But what if your backups aren't as safe as you think? This is the single biggest point of failure for even the most experienced Bitcoin owners.

Imagine these common disasters:

  • A house fire destroys the paper where you wrote down your seed phrase.
  • A flood rusts the metal plate you stamped your words on, making it unreadable.
  • A thief finds your "secure" hiding spot and steals your backup.
  • A hacker gains access to the encrypted file on your computer where you stored a digital copy.

If any of these happen, your Bitcoin could be lost forever. A professional review helps you see these risks before they become a reality.
