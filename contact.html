Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum Bitcoin holding for your services?

While we work with families at various wealth levels, our comprehensive estate planning services are optimized for those with $10M+ in Bitcoin holdings. For smaller portfolios, we offer educational resources and group consultations.

How long does the estate planning process take?

A comprehensive Bitcoin estate plan typically takes 60-90 days to implement, including legal structure creation, KEEP Protocol implementation, and family education. Emergency planning can be expedited to 30 days.

Do you provide ongoing support after implementation?

Yes, all our clients receive ongoing support including annual reviews, legislative updates, and access to our client portal. Premium clients also receive 24/7 emergency support and quarterly strategy sessions.

Can you work with my existing legal team?

Absolutely. We regularly collaborate with family offices, tax advisors, and estate attorneys. Our KEEP Protocol certification program also enables your existing advisors to become Bitcoin-proficient.
