The Continuity Problem

You accepted trusteeship of a trust that holds Bitcoin. Now what?

Unlike stocks, bonds, or real estate, Bitcoin doesn't sit quietly in a custodian's vault. It requires active technical management — and that management must continue flawlessly for decades, across personnel changes, technology upgrades, and unforeseen crises.

  • What happens when the hardware wallet's firmware becomes obsolete?
  • What happens when a keyholder resigns, dies, or becomes incapacitated?
  • What happens when a beneficiary requests a distribution and you don't know how to execute it?
  • What happens when the trust's creator — the only one who understood the setup — is gone?
  • What happens when you're sued for breach of fiduciary duty due to a technical failure you couldn't have prevented?

These aren't hypotheticals. They're the predictable failure modes of every Bitcoin trust without a continuity plan.

The Solution: A Technical Fiduciary Partner

Firm6102 acts as the Technical Fiduciary for Bitcoin trusts. We handle all technical operations so you can fulfill your fiduciary duties without becoming a cryptocurrency expert.

  • Quarterly "heartbeat" verification of all keys and hardware
  • Hardware rotation before device obsolescence
  • Keyholder succession management when personnel change
  • Annual succession fire drills to test recovery procedures
  • Technical execution of beneficiary distributions
  • Documentation for fiduciary defense and audit trails

You maintain legal authority. We provide the technical execution layer. The trust operates seamlessly across decades.
