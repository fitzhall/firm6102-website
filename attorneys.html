The Problem

Attorneys are drafting 21st-century trusts with 20th-century technical execution.

Your client holds $5 million in Bitcoin. You draft an irrevocable trust with sophisticated asset protection provisions. The legal architecture is sound.

But who executes the technical side?

  • The client sets up a hardware wallet from a YouTube tutorial
  • Seed phrases are stored in a safe deposit box — accessible during probate
  • No documentation of the custody architecture
  • The trustee has never touched a hardware wallet
  • A single point of failure could cause total loss

When something goes wrong — and with Bitcoin, something will eventually go wrong — the question becomes: Who bears the liability?

The Solution: Technical Due Diligence

Firm6102 provides Technical Due Diligence Reports for every trust you settle that holds digital assets. We become your technical execution partner — ensuring the custody architecture matches the legal architecture.

  • Multisig design aligned with trust provisions
  • Key distribution matching beneficiary structure
  • Hash-binding between trust deed and technical implementation
  • Chain of custody documentation for fiduciary defense
  • Qualified digital asset executor on standby

When a judge asks how your client's Bitcoin is secured — or when a beneficiary claims they can't access their inheritance — you have documented proof of institutional-grade technical execution.
